Shipping & Delivery Policy

The Desi N Pass is a 100% digital product. There are no physical items to ship.

Upon successful completion of your payment, your personalized digital Desi N Pass will be generated and delivered directly to the email address you provided during checkout.

Delivery Timeframe: All deliveries are electronic and are typically completed within a few hours of your purchase. Please allow up to 24 hours for processing. If you have not received your pass after 24 hours, please check your spam/junk folder before contacting us.
